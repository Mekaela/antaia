Making data simpler for farmers and policymakers

Antaia gives farmers and public agencies clear insights into farm-level and bioregional performance across agro-economic, environmental, and social indicators.

By transforming mandatory “Cadernos de Campo” records (with detailed, timestamped inputs) into actionable, geo-anchored data, it generates privacy-preserving regional reports—such as disease incidence and input intensity—while enabling farmers to access anonymized benchmarks and practice-level guidance.

For governments, ANTAIA accelerates the identification of effective practices and incentive levers, allowing targeted, timely support. The platform is modular and expandable, enabling progressively more sophisticated analyses over time; in Portugal alone, it could serve an estimated 120,000–160,000 farmers.
